ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

Innovation enhances the creativity and design thinking processes of organizations, leading to business success. The MCCIIE is hosting a 2-day International Conference on "Innovation and Entrepreneurship" to bring together leading academicians, researchers, entrepreneurs, and organizations to share their experiences and research findings. This conference will provide an interdisciplinary platform to discuss recent innovations, trends, and challenges in the fields of innovation and entrepreneurship

Objectives of the Conference

1. Discuss the role of innovation and incubation in entrepreneurship.

2. Encourage startup culture among students and faculty.

3. Devise strategies for building a sustainable business model.

4. Create awareness about government support and industry academia linkage in promoting entrepreneurial culture

Key Highlights

1. Unveiling Tomorrow's Trends: Our discussions dive into futurefocused insights that equip attendees with the tools to navigate the evolving entrepreneurial landscape

2. Expert-Led Sessions: Immerse in interactive workshops and talks delivered by accomplished leaders, offering unparalleled learning experiences.

3. Global Networking: Forge connections with industry titans and peers from around the globe, creating an ecosystem of collaboration and mentorship.

4. Igniting Creativity: Engage in dialogues that unlock innovative viewpoints, inspiring attendees to think beyond conventional boundaries.

5. Path to Growth: Equip the attendees with actionable strategies, unlocking avenues for accelerating business growth and success

ABOUT Mount Carmel College

Mount Carmel College, Autonomous, Bangalore, established in 1948, is dedicated to the empowerment of women through education. Managed by the Carmelite Sisters of St. Teresa (CSST), the college has set benchmarks in quality education and has empowered generations of women to become agents of change in society. Affiliated with Bangalore University and Bengaluru Central University, the college is recognized under 2(f) and 12(b) of the UGC Act. Granted autonomous status in 2005, MCC has been accredited by NAAC with an ‘A+’ grade in the last round of accreditation in 2019. The college offers 36 undergraduate and 12 postgraduate programs, with nearly 7,000 students enrolled, making it one of the top-ranked institutions in India

ABOUT Mount Carmel Centre forInnovation,Incubation and Entrepreneurship (MCCIIE)

MCCIIE is an initiative by Mount Carmel College to foster a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students, faculty, and alumni. The center aims to transform business ideas into reality by developing individual talent, skills, and personality. It provides a supportive entrepreneurial environment that accelerates the development of start up companies through various resources and services. The main goal of MCCIIE is to produce successful firms that are financially viable and independent.
