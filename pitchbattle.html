PHASE 1 - APPLICATION AND INITIAL SELECTION

JUNE 1 - JUNE 30

Announcement and Call for Applications

Start Date: June 1

End Date: June 30

Objective:

To attract and accept applications from startups and entrepreneurs globally.

Details:

    ⦿
    Promote the Pitch Battle through various channels (social media, email newsletters, partner organizations).
    ⦿
    Provide clear guidelines and criteria for application.
    ⦿
    Set up an online application portal for submissions.

Application Submission

Last Date: June 30

Objective:

To collect more than 100 applications from interested participants.

Details:

    ⦿
    Ensure the application portal is user-friendly and accessible.
    ⦿
    Collect necessary information such as business idea, team details, market analysis, and pitch deck.

PHASE 2 - EVALUATION AND MENTORSHIP

JUNE 15 - AUGUST 31

Initial Evaluation

Start Date: June 15

End Date: July 15

Objective:

To evaluate the submitted applications and select the top 100 startups for mentorship.

Details:

    ⦿
    Form an evaluation committee comprising industry experts and investors.
    ⦿
    Use a scoring system to assess the viability, innovation, and potential of each startup.
    ⦿
    Notify the selected 100 startups.

Mentorship Program

Start Date: July 15

End Date: August 15

Objective:

To provide mentorship to the selected 100 startups to refine their ideas and prepare for the next phase.

Details:

    ⦿
    Collaborate with the Mentoring Club USA to provide experienced mentors.
    ⦿
    Provide mentorship guidelines and documents from EntreCon Global.
    ⦿
    Conduct regular mentoring sessions and collect Mentorship Analysis Reports.

Post-Mentorship Evaluation

Start Date: August 15

End Date: August 31

Objective:

To evaluate the progress of the mentored startups and select the top 75 for further consideration

Details:

    ⦿
    Review the Mentorship Analysis Reports submitted by mentors
    ⦿
    Use the evaluation criteria to shortlist 75 startups.
    ⦿
    Notify the selected startups

PHASE 3 - FINAL SELECTION AND PITCH BATTLE

SEPTEMBER 1 - SEPTEMBER 20

Final Evaluation

Start Date: September 1

End Date: September 10

Objective:

To assess the top 75 startups and shortlist the top 30 for the final Pitch Battle.

Details:

    ⦿
    Based on the analysis and progress reports from Phase 2, evaluate the startups.
    ⦿
    Select the top 30 startups for the Pitch Battle.
    ⦿
    Notify the selected startups.

Preparation for Pitch Battle

Start Date: September 10

End Date: September 20

Objective:

To prepare the top 30 startups for the final Pitch Battle.

Details:

    ⦿
    Provide detailed guidelines and documents for the Pitch Battle.
    ⦿
    Conduct mock pitch sessions and provide feedback.

Pitch Battle Event

Date: September 26

Objective:

To conduct the final Pitch Battle and identify the winning startups.

Details:

    ⦿
    Each startup presents their pitch to a panel of investors, VCs, and industry experts.
    ⦿
    Evaluate pitches based on innovation, feasibility, market potential, and presentation.
    ⦿
    Select the winners and announce the results.
